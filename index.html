Quick Import from Drexel Learn

Go to Drexel Learn, open your course, go to Assignments or Calendar, select all the text on the page (Ctrl+A / Cmd+A), copy it (Ctrl+C / Cmd+C), and paste it below. The dashboard will automatically find your assignments and due dates.

How to get your assignments

  • Step 1: Log into Drexel Learn
  • Step 2: Open a course
  • Step 3: Go to Activities → Assignments (or Quizzes, Discussions)
  • Step 4: Press Cmd+A (select all), then Cmd+C (copy)
  • Step 5: Come back here and paste (Cmd+V) in the box above
